LIGHTS OUT!

Press SHUFFLE to Start!

Games Rules

The game consists of a '5x5' grid of lights. When the game starts, a random pattern of these lights is switched on. Pressing any of the lights will toggle it and the adjacent lights on and off. The goal of the puzzle is to switch all the lights off, preferably with as few button presses as possible.

How to Play!

(1) - Start the game by pressing the Shuffle button.
(2) - Press a light to toggle it and the adjacent lights on and off. When you press a light which is on, that light will turn off, and the lights around it will also toggle on or off in a fixed pattern, (see image below).
(3) - If you need help - Press the Show Solution button, to see which boxes to press, (highlighted in gold). To turn help off - Press the Hide Solution button.
(4) - The game will end when all lights are off, a 'Congratulations' message will then appear on screen.
(5) - To start a new game at any time - Click the Shuffle button.
